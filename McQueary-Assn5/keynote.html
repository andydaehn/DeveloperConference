Phil Spencer

Head of Microsoft's Xbox Division

Spencer joined Microsoft in 1988 as an intern and has worked in a number of technical roles.
In March 2014, Spencer took over as head of Microsoft's Xbox Division. In this keynote, you will
learn how big tech companies, like Microsoft, are incorporating training technologies found at this conference in everyday use.

Palmer Luckey

Founder of Oculus VR

Luckey is the "face of virtual reality in gaming" and a celebrity among VR enthusiasts. In this keynote session, you will take a journey into the coming world of VR and explore how VR is shaping the future of content. You will learn why new technologies require us to rething our current narratives, and how VR is changing the definition of "story" itself.
